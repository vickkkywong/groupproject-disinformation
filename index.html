What is Disinformation?

False information is information that is untrue.

False information has a huge negative impact.

The authenticity of reports is one of the elements of news value and the life of news. No matter what kind of media, it must follow the rules of news authenticity. However, the communication characteristics of the Internet itself provide a breeding ground for the generation of false information. The damage caused by the spread of false information to enterprises or individuals is even worse than that of traditional media. The Internet is highly open and interactive. Any website can produce and publish information, providing a platform for all dissemination of information and expression of opinions. opened up an almost unrestricted space. It is this unlimited freedom that allows some information falsifiers and rumor spreaders to make irresponsible remarks online, or intentionally s pread false information and create chaos.

The influence of the Internet in today's society is rapidly expanding, so the Internet has evolved into an engine for rapid growth of enterprises. No matter what industry you are in, the future is inseparable from the Internet. Just as the emergence of print media opened up a new era in the history of news communication, the Internet, known as the "fourth media", has achieved another revolution in the history of human communication.
